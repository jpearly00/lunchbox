What's in our grab-and-go food. Scan, check, and eat with confidence.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Heads up — we're still filling this in

Ingredient and allergen details are being finalized and double-checked, item by item. Anything marked DRAFT isn't confirmed yet. If you have an allergy and don't see what you need, just ask a team member — we're glad to help.

Cross-contamination is always possible

We prep, cook, and clean in the optimal order to keep things separated — but this is a single, shared, multi-use kitchen and store. We can't guarantee any item is free of contact with other ingredients or allergens.

No gluten-free at this time

We do not currently offer gluten-free items, and we can't guarantee any item is free of wheat or gluten. We'll update this page if that changes.

Why our sandwiches use vegan aioli

We spread vegan aioli on our sandwiches because we think it's higher quality and better tasting — plain and simple. (It also happens to be quite a bit more expensive than the usual stuff. We decided it's worth it.)
