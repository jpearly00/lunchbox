How it works

1

Order ahead

Put your order in below, ideally the day before your tour. Go with a menu choice, make a tweak, or build your own.

2

We pack it

Made fresh at Coyote's, 805 N Main St, labeled with your name and tour date, with snacks and drinks packed to go with it.

3

It rides with the tour

Sand & Stars picks it up and brings it along automatically, or the tour stops by Coyote's on the way out. Either way, it's handled.

The Sandwiches

All sandwiches come on a soft white ciabatta roll by default, or ask for sliced sourdough. Swap, drop, or mix & match anything you see. PLAIN (no condiments) always available. Snacks and drinks come along with every lunch. Got a food allergy or dietary need? Give us a call at 435-633-1145 and we'll take care of you.
